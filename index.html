About us

In e-commerce, a virtual shopping cart is a software application that allows online shoppers to add products to their cart while browsing a website.

Users can review the items in their cart, modify quantities, and proceed to checkout when ready to make a purchase. Shopping carts typically store information about selected products, quantities, and prices until the user completes the transaction.
